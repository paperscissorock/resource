re:defined

 

re:source is a forum dedicated mostly to those less well charted areas of music from Poland and its neighbouring countries. We are interested in music and other cultural matters. We aim to provide a space here that helps support them and perhaps, because we are rigidly keeping all the text to English, encourage more people to take notice of what is going on in these territories.

 

We want more people to get involved, too. We are looking for interviews and reviews, or simply articles dedicated to artists or topics that may dovetail with our interests, city reports (concerning local artists, clubs, labels, radio shows, etc.), and indeed anything that we feel will help expand on our main idea. If you have anything of this nature or would like to get involved in any other way, please contact us via the re:spond page. No unsolicited material will be published, however.

 

re:source will continue to steadily grow, so please keep checking in on this website for more additions and/or information concerning other things we are involved in.
